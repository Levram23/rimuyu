About Rimuyo Tours

Your Local Guides to Lake Titicaca

Founded by local families who have called Lake Titicaca home for generations, Rimuyo Tours offers authentic experiences that go beyond typical tourism.

Our name "Rimuyo" comes from Quechua, meaning "to speak together" – reflecting our commitment to cultural exchange and meaningful connections between travelers and our communities.
